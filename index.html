ABOUT ME

My name is Leigh Adams and I am passionate web developer with a penchant for crafting engaging and innovative online experiences. With 2 years of experience in the ever-evolving realm of web development, I thrive on turning ideas into reality through clean, efficient code and intuitive design.

I found my way into web development from doing art and printmaking, a change completely out of left field. But that background has been instrumental in my approach to web design + development. Over the past couple years, I've advanced my skills in a variety of programming languages and frameworks, including Javascript, PHP, MySQL, React, React Native, nodeJs, and Expo, allowing me to tackle projects with versatility and expertise.

Beyond the technical aspects, I take pride in my ability to understand the users' needs and translate them into dynamic, user-centric websites and applications. Whether it's building responsive layouts or implementing cutting-edge features, I'm committed to delivering solutions that not only meet but exceed expectations.

When I'm not immersed in lines of code, you can find me exploring the latest trends in web development, working on my own pieces/ projects. or seeking inspiration from art, design, and nature. I'm a firm believer in lifelong learning and continuously strive to expand my skill set to stay ahead in this dynamic industry. I'm beyond excited to make your acquaintance and achieve greatness with you!

To Do List App

  • purpose: An app that's designed to track completed and uncompleted tasks with set deadlines
  • tech: react native, nodeJS, Expo
  • skills: expo for cross-platforming, react native UI components, real time updates with state management
  • problem: separating the completed and uncompleted tasks from each other, I had to completely switch methods and just filter out the comleted and uncompleted tasks it worked. The best solutions are simplest ones, and that's the biggest thing I learned in this project: always go for the simplest answer.
